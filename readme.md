# 📊 Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🧠 Overview
This Power BI project focuses on analyzing key sales metrics to provide deep business insights. It enables tracking sales trends, understanding product/customer performance, and monitoring essential KPIs in a clean, interactive format.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 📈 **Sales Trends**: Month-over-month growth and seasonal patterns identified clearly.
- 💸 **Top Products**: Best-performing products in terms of revenue and volume identified through data.
- 🧍‍♂️ **Customer Performance**: Analyze customer behavior, purchase value, and retention.
- 📊 **Returns Impact**: Evaluate how product returns affect total sales and performance.

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used
- **Power BI Desktop**
- Power Query for data transformation
- DAX for calculated measures and KPIs
- Drillthrough filters and slicers for interactivity

## 🕐 Time Taken
- **Data Preparation & Cleaning**: ~2 hours  
- **Dashboard Design & Layout**: ~3 hours  
- **DAX Measures & KPIs**: ~2 hours  
- **Interactivity Setup (Drillthrough, Filters)**: ~1.5 hours  
- **Final Polishing & Testing**: ~1 hour  

**➡️ Total Time: ~9.5 hours**
